AREAS OF PRACTICE

01 ILLUSTRATION & ART DIRECTION

I create illustrations with a strong visual identity, combining creativity, concept and detail. My work focuses on translating ideas and emotions into visual narratives that feel unique and intentional. Each project is developed from an initial concept and adapted to the needs, style and values of the brand or project.

02 AUDIOVISUAL DESIGN & MOTION

I design and produce audiovisual pieces that merge motion, design and storytelling. From animated visuals to video content, I focus on rhythm, composition and atmosphere to create engaging pieces that capture attention and communicate messages in a clear and expressive way.

03 BRANDING & VISUAL CONTENT

I develop visual identities and content systems that help brands build a strong and recognizable presence. My approach combines aesthetic consistency with creative direction, ensuring that each visual element aligns with the brand’s personality across different platforms and formats.

04 VISUAL STORYTELLING & CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT

I work on the development of visual concepts and narratives, shaping ideas from early stages into coherent visual stories. Through experimentation and creative direction, I help transform abstract concepts into expressive visuals that communicate meaning, atmosphere and identity.
